WELLINGTON

Compact, creative, and charming — Wellington is New Zealand’s capital of cool. With its harborside walks, indie shops, coffee culture, and cinematic landscapes, it’s a small city with a big soul.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,600 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Wellington Airport, transfer to hotel
  • 3:00PM – Stroll along Oriental Bay and enjoy seaside cafés
  • 7:30PM – Welcome dinner in Cuba Street's lively district

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Visit Te Papa Tongarewa (National Museum)
  • 12:30PM – Lunch at the waterfront market
  • 2:30PM – Cable car ride to the Botanic Gardens
  • 7:00PM – Craft beer tasting night at local breweries

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Morning hike to Mount Victoria Lookout
  • 11:00AM – Coffee tour of Wellington’s best cafés
  • 3:00PM – Weta Workshop guided movie magic tour
  • 8:00PM – Fine dining with harbor views

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Explore Zealandia Eco-Sanctuary
  • 11:30AM – Farewell brunch in a garden café
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
