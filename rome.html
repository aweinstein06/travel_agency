ROME

From ancient ruins to rooftop aperitivos — Rome is a living museum wrapped in golden light. Stroll past the Colosseum, toss a coin in the Trevi Fountain, and fall in love with la dolce vita.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,800 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Fiumicino Airport, transfer to hotel
  • 3:00PM – Espresso and gelato walk near Piazza Navona
  • 7:30PM – Rooftop welcome dinner overlooking the Pantheon

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Skip-the-line tour of the Colosseum & Roman Forum
  • 12:30PM – Lunch in the Monti district
  • 3:00PM – Visit to the Vatican Museums and Sistine Chapel
  • 8:00PM – Aperitivo in Trastevere

● Day 3

  • 10:00AM – Guided tour of the Borghese Gallery and Gardens
  • 1:00PM – Pizza-making class with wine tasting
  • 4:00PM – Free time for shopping on Via del Corso
  • 8:30PM – Candlelit dinner by the Tiber River

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Morning visit to Campo de’ Fiori & local market
  • 11:30AM – Farewell cappuccino at a historic Roman café
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
