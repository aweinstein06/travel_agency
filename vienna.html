VIENNA

Timeless and elegant, Vienna is a city of music, art, and coffeehouse charm. Wander imperial palaces, listen to Mozart where he lived, and indulge in strudel beneath crystal chandeliers.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,750 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Vienna International Airport, transfer to central hotel
  • 3:00PM – Walk through the historic Innere Stadt
  • 7:00PM – Candlelit dinner with live classical music

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Visit to Schönbrunn Palace and gardens
  • 12:30PM – Lunch at a traditional Viennese Gasthaus
  • 3:00PM – Tour the Belvedere Museum (Klimt’s *The Kiss*)
  • 8:00PM – Attend a Mozart & Strauss concert at Kursalon Wien

● Day 3

  • 10:00AM – St. Stephen’s Cathedral tour + climb to the tower
  • 1:00PM – Coffee & cake at Café Central
  • 3:00PM – Optional Spanish Riding School show or shopping on Graben
  • 7:30PM – Gourmet dinner at Steirereck (2 Michelin stars)

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Leisure walk through Stadtpark
  • 11:30AM – Farewell lunch at Naschmarkt
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
