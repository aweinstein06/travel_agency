ANKARA

Discover the vibrant capital of Türkiye, a unique blend of modernity and ancient heritage. From historic mosques to bustling markets and political landmarks, Ankara invites you to explore its rich cultural soul.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,800 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 10:00AM – Guided walking tour of the old town & Ankara Castle
  • 1:00PM – Turkish lunch in Hamamönü district
  • 3:00PM – Visit to the Museum of Anatolian Civilizations
  • 7:00PM – Dinner at a traditional Ottoman-style restaurant

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Visit Anıtkabir, the Mausoleum of Atatürk
  • 11:30AM – Coffee with a view at Atakule Tower
  • 2:00PM – Explore local markets and shop for handmade souvenirs
  • 8:00PM – Dinner & live music in Kızılay

● Day 3

  • 10:00AM – Turkish cooking class: meze, kebab, and baklava
  • 1:30PM – Relaxing spa/hammam experience
  • 4:00PM – Afternoon stroll in Gençlik Park
  • 7:30PM – Rooftop dinner with panoramic city views

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Breakfast at a historic cafe
  • 10:30AM – Visit to the CerModern Contemporary Arts Center
  • 1:00PM – Farewell lunch & airport transfer
