BANGKOK

A city that never sleeps — Bangkok combines ancient traditions, golden temples, buzzing markets, and world-class cuisine. This is the beating heart of Thailand, ready to be explored.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,600 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrival at Suvarnabhumi Airport, transfer to hotel
  • 3:00PM – Thai welcome massage and relaxation
  • 7:00PM – Dinner cruise on the Chao Phraya River

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Visit to Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew (Emerald Buddha)
  • 12:30PM – Street food lunch in Chinatown
  • 3:00PM – Explore Wat Arun & ferry ride
  • 6:00PM – Rooftop dinner at Vertigo or Mahanakhon SkyBar

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Floating Market & train market day trip
  • 2:00PM – Free time to shop in Siam Paragon / MBK
  • 7:30PM – Thai dance dinner show or Muay Thai experience

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Local temple blessing & flower market
  • 11:00AM – Coffee & brunch in Ari district
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
