SUVA

Suva is where tropical island charm meets lively Pacific culture. With oceanfront strolls, rainforest escapes, and vibrant markets, Fiji’s capital is a laid-back gateway to paradise.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,400 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Nausori Airport, welcome lei & transfer to resort
  • 3:00PM – Oceanfront walk and coconut welcome drink
  • 7:00PM – Sunset dinner with traditional Fijian music

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Guided visit to Suva Municipal Market & Colo-i-Suva rainforest
  • 1:00PM – Waterfall swim and picnic lunch
  • 4:00PM – Explore Fiji Museum and Thurston Gardens
  • 8:00PM – Indian-Fijian fusion dinner in downtown Suva

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Boat tour to nearby island + snorkeling
  • 12:00PM – Beach BBQ with fresh seafood & tropical fruit
  • 5:00PM – Return to Suva and optional Fijian kava ceremony
  • 8:30PM – Drinks and live music at a seaside bar

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Morning paddleboarding or yoga by the sea
  • 11:00AM – Brunch with island pastries and local jams
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
