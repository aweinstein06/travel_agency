SANTIAGO

Santiago is a city of energy and elegance — where the Andes meet avant-garde art, rooftop bars overlook colonial cathedrals, and every street tells a story of past and present.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,650 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrival at Santiago Airport, transfer to Lastarria hotel
  • 3:00PM – Explore Barrio Lastarria + coffee at a historic café
  • 7:30PM – Rooftop welcome dinner with Andes backdrop

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Guided city tour: Plaza de Armas, La Moneda, Bellas Artes
  • 12:30PM – Lunch at a mercado with Chilean specialties
  • 3:00PM – Visit to Museo de la Memoria (Human Rights Museum)
  • 8:00PM – Wine tasting and dinner in Bellavista

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Day trip to Casablanca Valley for vineyard tour
  • 1:00PM – Wine-pairing lunch at Bodega Catrala
  • 5:30PM – Return to Santiago, sunset at Sky Costanera
  • 9:00PM – Optional pisco tasting or jazz night

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Stroll up Cerro Santa Lucía
  • 11:00AM – Artisan shopping at Pueblito Los Dominicos
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
