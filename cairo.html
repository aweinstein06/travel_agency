CAIRO

Step into the cradle of civilization. From the Pyramids of Giza to the bustling bazaars of Khan el-Khalili, Cairo blends ancient wonders with vibrant modern life.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,700 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrival at Cairo International Airport, private transfer
  • 3:00PM – Welcome tea at hotel & brief local orientation
  • 7:00PM – Dinner overlooking the Nile River

● Day 2

  • 8:00AM – Full-day tour: Pyramids of Giza, Sphinx, and Saqqara
  • 1:00PM – Camel ride & lunch at a local Bedouin camp
  • 6:30PM – Evening felucca ride on the Nile

● Day 3

  • 9:00AM – Egyptian Museum & King Tut’s treasures
  • 12:30PM – Explore Islamic Cairo: mosques, alleyways, ancient gates
  • 3:00PM – Shopping in Khan el-Khalili Bazaar
  • 8:00PM – Sound and Light show at the pyramids

● Day 4

  • 8:30AM – Visit to Coptic Cairo & Hanging Church
  • 11:00AM – Egyptian breakfast in Zamalek
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
