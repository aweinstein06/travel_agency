MADRID

Madrid is bold, elegant, and alive — where royal palaces meet tapas bars, flamenco ignites the night, and plazas buzz with life. A perfect city to explore by day and savor by night.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,850 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrival at Madrid-Barajas, transfer to city center
  • 3:00PM – Stroll through Plaza Mayor and Mercado de San Miguel
  • 8:00PM – Tapas dinner and welcome drinks in La Latina

● Day 2

  • 9:30AM – Guided tour of the Royal Palace & Almudena Cathedral
  • 12:30PM – Lunch near Gran Vía and shopping time
  • 4:00PM – Visit to the Prado Museum
  • 9:00PM – Flamenco show with wine & tapas

● Day 3

  • 10:00AM – Morning rowboat ride at Retiro Park
  • 12:30PM – Rooftop lunch with panoramic views of Madrid
  • 3:00PM – Explore the Reina Sofía Museum (Picasso’s *Guernica*)
  • 7:30PM – Sunset drinks in the Malasaña district

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Brunch at El Rastro flea market (Sundays)
  • 11:30AM – Walk along the Madrid Río
  • 1:00PM – Farewell lunch & airport transfer
