VANCOUVER

Where ocean meets mountains, and modernity meets nature — Vancouver is a haven for food lovers, hikers, skiers, and creatives. Fresh air, global flavor, and West Coast vibes await.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,850 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at YVR, transfer to downtown hotel
  • 3:00PM – Explore Gastown: cobblestone streets & steam clock
  • 7:00PM – Pacific seafood dinner on the waterfront

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Bike ride around Stanley Park Seawall
  • 12:00PM – Sushi lunch on Robson Street
  • 3:00PM – Visit to Granville Island Public Market
  • 8:00PM – Rooftop cocktails in Yaletown

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Day trip to Capilano Suspension Bridge & Grouse Mountain
  • 1:00PM – Lunch with mountain views
  • 4:00PM – Return and spa afternoon at the hotel
  • 7:30PM – Dinner at a farm-to-table bistro

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Morning stroll along English Bay Beach
  • 11:30AM – Farewell brunch in Kitsilano
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
