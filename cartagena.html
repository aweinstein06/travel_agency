CARTAGENA

Cartagena is Colombia’s crown jewel — a walled city of romance and rhythm. With vibrant facades, beachy breezes, and Afro-Caribbean soul, this city invites you to slow down and dance through time.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,400 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Rafael Núñez Airport, transfer to historic center
  • 3:00PM – Welcome drinks on a colonial rooftop
  • 7:00PM – Dinner at a coastal fusion restaurant inside the walled city

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Walking tour of the old town & Getsemaní graffiti streets
  • 12:00PM – Ceviche lunch at a local café
  • 3:00PM – Visit to Castillo San Felipe fortress
  • 6:30PM – Sunset cocktails at Café del Mar

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Day trip to the Rosario Islands (snorkeling & beach lunch)
  • 5:00PM – Return & relax by the hotel pool
  • 8:00PM – Live music and dancing in Plaza de la Trinidad

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Visit to Bazurto Market with local guide
  • 11:30AM – Fresh fruit tasting & artisan shopping
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
