MARRAKESH

Step into the red city — Marrakesh is a feast for the senses. Snake charmers, orange blossom, tiled courtyards, spice-filled souks, and glowing lanterns create an unforgettable Moroccan experience.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,500 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Marrakesh Menara Airport, private riad transfer
  • 3:00PM – Welcome mint tea & traditional hammam experience
  • 8:00PM – Moroccan dinner with live Gnawa music

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Explore the medina: Bahia Palace, Koutoubia Mosque, and souks
  • 12:30PM – Lunch in a rooftop café overlooking the market
  • 3:00PM – Visit to the Majorelle Garden & Yves Saint Laurent Museum
  • 7:00PM – Sunset drinks at El Fenn terrace

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Day trip to the Agafay Desert: camel ride & lunch under a tent
  • 6:00PM – Return & optional spa treatment or souk shopping
  • 9:00PM – Dinner in a traditional riad with belly dancing show

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Visit to a local artisan cooperative (ceramics or textiles)
  • 11:00AM – Moroccan brunch with pastries and mint tea
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
