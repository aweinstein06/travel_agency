ZANZIBAR

Welcome to Zanzibar — where white-sand beaches, spice plantations, and Swahili culture create a getaway full of soul and serenity. Sail the Indian Ocean, explore Stone Town, and unwind in barefoot luxury.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,700 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Abeid Amani Karume Airport, welcome coconut drink
  • 3:00PM – Relax and swim at beach resort
  • 7:30PM – Dinner on the beach with live taarab music

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Guided tour of historic Stone Town
  • 12:30PM – Swahili lunch in a rooftop café
  • 3:00PM – Spice plantation experience & tasting
  • 6:30PM – Sunset dhow cruise with snacks & drinks

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Snorkeling tour to Mnemba Atoll
  • 1:00PM – Seafood beach BBQ on a sandbank
  • 4:00PM – Optional massage or visit to Jozani Forest
  • 8:00PM – Dinner under the stars

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Morning yoga or ocean dip
  • 11:00AM – Farewell breakfast with tropical fruits
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
