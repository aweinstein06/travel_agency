JOHANNESBURG

Johannesburg is South Africa’s beating heart — a vibrant metropolis of resilience, history, and reinvention. From Soweto’s stories to Sandton’s sparkle, Jozi offers powerful experiences at every turn.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,750 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrival at OR Tambo International, transfer to hotel
  • 3:00PM – Orientation walk through Rosebank or Melrose Arch
  • 7:00PM – Welcome dinner with live jazz in Maboneng

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Guided tour of the Apartheid Museum & Constitution Hill
  • 1:00PM – Traditional South African lunch in Soweto
  • 3:00PM – Visit to Nelson Mandela’s House & Vilakazi Street
  • 6:00PM – Sunset cocktails at The Leonardo’s rooftop bar

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Day safari at Pilanesberg National Park (Big Five spotting)
  • 6:00PM – Return to Johannesburg & dinner at Marble Restaurant

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Brunch & shopping at Neighbourgoods Market
  • 11:30AM – Street art tour of Braamfontein
  • 1:00PM – Farewell lunch & airport transfer
