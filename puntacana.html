PUNTA CANA

A tropical playground in the Dominican Republic — Punta Cana is all about crystal-clear waters, all-inclusive bliss, Caribbean rhythms, and long lazy days under swaying palms.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,200 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Punta Cana Airport, tropical welcome drink
  • 3:00PM – Beach check-in and afternoon swim
  • 7:00PM – Seafood dinner under the stars

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Catamaran cruise & snorkeling at Isla Catalina
  • 1:00PM – Onboard BBQ and open bar
  • 5:00PM – Return to resort and sunset beach walk
  • 8:00PM – Dominican-themed buffet with live merengue

● Day 3

  • 10:00AM – Spa morning: coconut scrub and beach massage
  • 1:00PM – Lunch at beachfront grill
  • 3:00PM – Optional zip-lining or cenote swim excursion
  • 7:30PM – Fine dining at Passion by Martín Berasategui

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Sunrise yoga or paddleboarding session
  • 11:00AM – Tropical fruit brunch & oceanfront photos
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
