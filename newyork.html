NEW YORK CITY

Welcome to the city that never sleeps — New York is a dizzying mix of energy, style, culture, and skyline views. From Central Park to SoHo, it's not just a place — it’s a lifestyle.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €3,600 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at JFK or LGA, private transfer to Manhattan
  • 3:00PM – Walk through Central Park and Upper West Side
  • 8:00PM – Dinner in Tribeca and skyline drinks at a rooftop bar

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island ferry tour
  • 1:00PM – Lunch at Eataly near the World Trade Center
  • 3:00PM – Visit the 9/11 Memorial and One World Observatory
  • 7:00PM – Broadway show in Times Square

● Day 3

  • 10:00AM – Explore the MET or the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)
  • 1:00PM – Brunch in SoHo and shopping in Nolita
  • 4:00PM – Sunset walk across the Brooklyn Bridge
  • 8:00PM – Jazz night in Greenwich Village

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Hudson Yards & the Vessel experience
  • 11:30AM – Final bagels or pancakes at a classic NYC diner
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
