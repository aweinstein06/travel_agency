RIO DE JANEIRO

A city of contrast and charisma — Rio blends golden beaches, lush jungle, samba beats, and Christ the Redeemer above it all. Whether you crave carnival or calm, Rio delivers both.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €2,900 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Galeão International Airport, transfer to hotel
  • 3:00PM – Walk along Copacabana and enjoy a fresh coconut
  • 8:00PM – Welcome caipirinhas and dinner with beach views

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Visit Christ the Redeemer & Tijuca National Forest
  • 12:30PM – Lunch at a churrascaria (Brazilian BBQ)
  • 3:00PM – Explore the Selarón Steps & Santa Teresa neighborhood
  • 7:30PM – Samba show at Rio Scenarium

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Cable car to Sugarloaf Mountain for sunrise views
  • 10:30AM – Beach time at Ipanema & lunch at a local kiosk
  • 3:00PM – Optional hang-gliding or botanical garden visit
  • 8:00PM – Rooftop dinner with panoramic city views

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Morning visit to Feira Hippie de Ipanema (Sundays)
  • 11:30AM – Fresh fruit brunch & live Bossa Nova music
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
