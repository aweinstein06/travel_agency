PAPEETE

Welcome to Tahiti’s dreamy capital — Papeete is a vibrant fusion of island spirit and French flair. Lush markets, dancing palms, coral lagoons, and golden sunsets await you in paradise.

4 Days and 3 Nights Getaway

Price starts at €4,200 per person (excluding flights)

● Day 1

  • 12:00PM – Arrive at Faa’a International Airport, fresh lei welcome
  • 3:00PM – Check-in to your beachfront bungalow and relax
  • 7:00PM – Polynesian dinner show with fire dancing

● Day 2

  • 9:00AM – Guided tour of Papeete Market & Pearl Museum
  • 12:30PM – Lunch with a view at a lagoon-side café
  • 3:00PM – Outrigger canoe ride & snorkeling at the reef
  • 6:00PM – Sunset cocktails over Moorea views

● Day 3

  • 8:00AM – Jeep safari into Tahiti’s lush interior
  • 1:00PM – Picnic by waterfalls & river swim
  • 5:00PM – Spa treatment with monoi oils
  • 8:00PM – Dinner at Le Coco’s with ocean breeze

● Day 4

  • 9:00AM – Visit black sand beach & farewell photoshoot
  • 11:30AM – Tropical brunch & fresh coconut juice
  • 1:00PM – Check-out & airport transfer
